About Pathfork

Pathfork is made, maintained, and grown by Tynan Burke, who had some free time and wanted a cloud-based tool just like it to use in his own writing.

Pathfork is written in Go and served by Heroku. You can find the code on Github if you'd like to take a peek or contribute. It is not perfect and I would love some help.

Running Pathfork is not free. I'm more than happy to do it, but if you want to support me on Patreon (even a dollar!) that would be great. I also keep a BTC wallet at 14BgUAk3mq24szBDfNs2WQyMVXR4tHmQQz.

Happy writing!
